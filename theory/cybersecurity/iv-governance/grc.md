# GRC

**Governance Activities**

* Define organizational objectives, policies, and strategic direction.
* Establish roles, responsibilities, and decision-making structures.
* Approve risk appetite and tolerances.
* Oversee major programs (security, IT, compliance).
* Monitor performance through KPIs/KRIs.
* Ensure alignment between business goals and IT/security initiatives.

***

**Risk Management Activities**

* Identify risks (operational, cybersecurity, financial, compliance).
* Analyze and assess risks (likelihood, impact).
* Prioritize risks based on risk appetite.
* Implement risk treatments (avoid, reduce, transfer, accept).
* Perform continuous monitoring of risks and controls.
* Maintain risk registers and update risk assessments regularly.
* Conduct incident analysis and lessons learned.

***

**Compliance Activities**

* Identify applicable laws, standards, and regulatory requirements.
* Map controls to compliance requirements.
* Conduct internal audits and assessments.
* Maintain evidence repositories for audits.
* Ensure policies and procedures meet regulatory expectations.
* Handle compliance reporting and certification processes.
* Provide compliance training and awareness.

***

**Integrated GRC Activities**

* Align governance, risk, and compliance data to reduce duplication.
* Use GRC platforms to centralize policies, controls, risks, and evidence.
* Enable real-time reporting to executives and auditors.
* Coordinate cross-functional teams (IT, security, legal, risk).
* Support continuous improvement of controls and processes.
