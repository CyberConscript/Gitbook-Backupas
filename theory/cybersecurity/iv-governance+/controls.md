# Controls

Security controls should be linked to the business strategy by ensuring the information security strategy

considers business objectives.

Conducting risk assessments with a focus on critical business assets.

Helps identify potential risks and develop a security policy to address them.

If the business strategy includes expanding into new markets, the security strategy should include

measures to comply with local regulations and protect customer data in those markets.
